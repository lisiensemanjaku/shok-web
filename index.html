Key Features

Everything you need to connect and collaborate

Contact Details

Communicate via the email you signed in with or add your phone number to connect in your favourite messaging app.

Profile Customisation

Upload a profile picture, write a bio, and showcase your skills and interests.

Invite System

Generate invite codes to grow a trusted community. Earn referral rewards when they join.

Save & Sort Posts

Bookmark requests and filter the Feed by the criteria that interests you most.

Comment & Reply

Ask questions to clarify details directly on posts through threaded comments.

Expiry & Deadlines

Each post shows an expiry date indicating the deadline by when the task must be completed.

Edit & Delete

Edit post details or delete posts anytime.

Home Tab Sections

"Feed" displays only others' posts while "Your Posts" shows posts you've created.

My Bids Tab

Track posts you've bid on and view your active offers in one place.

Committed Credits

The credits that are the current offer in your active posts are temporarily blocked and can't be used for the offer in new posts.
