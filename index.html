How Shok works

A community where people genuinely help each other.

01

Welcome to Shok

A community where people genuinely help each other. Not a marketplace, not a service app — just real people showing up for one another.

02

Post what you need

Describe what you need, set the credits you're offering, and add #keywords so the right people can find it. You choose a deadline, and the post stays open until then.

03

Offer to help

Browse what neighbours need near you and place a bid — accept the credits offered or name your own price. The poster picks who helps, and you can message each other to sort out the rest.

04

Credits keep things fair

Everyone starts with some credits. Spend them to ask for help, they go to the helper whose bid you accept, and you earn them back by helping others. Invite someone and you both get a small boost.

05

Your community awaits

You're part of it now. Ask when you need a hand, give one when you can, and the community grows a little stronger every time.

Our Mission

Rebuilding the fabric of human connection through meaningful acts of kindness.

Reconnecting a disconnected world

In an era where social media platforms have paradoxically made us more isolated than ever, we believe it's time for a fundamental shift. While traditional social networks encourage endless scrolling and superficial interactions, they've created a generation that's more connected to screens than to the people around them.

Real help, real connection, real community

When the world feels fractured by conflict, economic strain, and widening divides, the power of genuine human connection has never been more critical. We're building an inclusive network built on trust, where every task you post and every favour you complete rekindles hope in humanity. This isn't about virtual likes or digital validation — it's about real people helping real people.

From strangers to friends through acts of kindness

We believe that true friendships are forged through shared experiences and mutual support, not through comments and reactions. When someone helps you move furniture, tutors your child, or simply lends a hand when you need it most, something magical happens — barriers break down, trust is built, and genuine connections are formed.

Transforming uncertainty into solidarity

Our platform creates meaningful interactions where favours come with real stakes, real rewards, and real people you can trust. Every credit spent, every task completed, and every moment of help transforms uncertainty into solidarity, one act of kindness at a time. Together, we're not just building an app — we're rebuilding the fabric of human connection.

All data is stored securely on our private servers, housed in EU-based data centres with appropriate technical and organizational safeguards.

Our App is not directed to children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. Security measures
